KaleidoSwap has secured a pre-seed investment from [Bitfinex Ventures](https://www.bitfinex.com/) and [Fulgur Ventures](https://fulgur.ventures/), two leading funds dedicated to advancing innovation in the Bitcoin ecosystem.

This investment will accelerate the development of our **[RGB](https://rgb.info/)-based decentralized exchange (DEX) on the [Lightning Network](https://lightning.network/)**, bringing us closer to redefining decentralized finance (DeFi) on Bitcoin.

Just days ago, [Tether](https://tether.io/) made an [announcement](https://tether.io/news/tether-to-launch-usdt-on-rgb-expanding-native-bitcoin-stablecoin-support/) about **USDT launching natively on RGB**. It's a move that aligns perfectly with our vision of enabling Bitcoin-native trading.

### Why KaleidoSwap: Stablecoins on Bitcoin are coming

At KaleidoSwap, we believe that **liquidity attracts volume**.

- **Bitcoin** is the world's most liquid digital asset and the most secure settlement layer.
- **Lightning** delivers scalability, privacy, and decentralization.
- **RGB** unlocks trustless smart contracts and asset issuance directly on Bitcoin.

By combining these pillars, we're building what we believe to be the **first truly native Bitcoin DEX**, a decentralized liquidity hub where Bitcoiners can access **trustless BTC–[USDT](https://tether.to/en/)** trading and yield opportunities without compromising Bitcoin's values.

> "Our mission is to create the go-to DEX for Bitcoin DeFi. With RGB on Lightning, we're enabling trustless, scalable trading of BTC against stablecoins, paving the way for a vibrant ecosystem of assets and financial applications." — **Walter Maffione, CEO, KaleidoSwap**

> "We are delighted to be an early investor into KaleidoSwap. We have been a long-time supporter of RGB and Lightning technologies, and are committed to making Bitcoin the most efficient transaction solution for everyone." — **Paolo Ardoino, CTO, Bitfinex**

> "KaleidoSwap's vision fully aligns with ours: build on Bitcoin, leverage Lightning for scalability, and use RGB for smart contracts. We continue to see enormous potential in a bitcoin-native technology stack and are proud to support the team in their mission." — **Oleg Mikhalsky, General Partner, Fulgur Ventures**

### About Our Investors: Leaders in the Bitcoin Ecosystem

**Bitfinex Ventures** is the investment arm of Bitfinex, one of the world's longest-standing digital asset exchanges. The firm supports projects that advance the Bitcoin ecosystem and the broader blockchain industry, with a focus on infrastructure, scalability, and financial innovation. By investing in early-stage ventures, Bitfinex Ventures fosters technologies that align with its mission to make Bitcoin the most efficient global transaction solution.

**Fulgur Ventures** is a venture capital firm focused on Bitcoin technology and its applications and dedicated to accelerating the adoption of bitcoin as financial infrastructure.

### About KaleidoSwap: Lightning DEX with Bitcoin-native stablecoins

KaleidoSwap is pioneering the first decentralized exchange (DEX) built natively on Bitcoin and the Lightning Network. Leveraging RGB, an advanced protocol for smart contracts and asset issuance on Bitcoin, KaleidoSwap enables **trustless BTC-USDT trading** and a broader ecosystem of Bitcoin-backed assets. With a focus on scalability, privacy, and decentralization, KaleidoSwap is on a mission to become the **gateway for Bitcoin-native DeFi**, enabling trustless Bitcoin swaps across all its layers.
