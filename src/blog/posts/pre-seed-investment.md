**Lugano, Switzerland — September 8, 2025**

We're excited to share a major milestone for KaleidoSwap: we have secured a pre-seed investment from **Bitfinex Ventures** and **Fulgur Ventures**, two leading funds dedicated to advancing innovation in the Bitcoin ecosystem.

This investment will accelerate the development of our **RGB-based decentralized exchange (DEX) on the Lightning Network**, bringing us closer to redefining decentralized finance (DeFi) on Bitcoin.

Just days ago, Tether announced that **USD₮ will launch natively on RGB** (August 28, 2025) — a move that aligns perfectly with our vision of enabling Bitcoin-native stablecoin trading.

### Why KaleidoSwap

At KaleidoSwap, we believe in a simple principle: **liquidity attracts volume**.

- **Bitcoin** is the world's most liquid digital asset and the most secure settlement layer.
- **Lightning** delivers scalability, privacy, and decentralization.
- **RGB** unlocks trustless smart contracts and asset issuance directly on Bitcoin.

By combining these pillars, we're building what we believe to be the **first truly native Bitcoin DEX** — a decentralized liquidity hub where Bitcoiners can access **trustless BTC–USDT trading** and yield opportunities without compromising Bitcoin's values.

> "Our mission is to create the go-to DEX for Bitcoin DeFi. With RGB on Lightning, we're enabling trustless, scalable trading of BTC against stablecoins — paving the way for a vibrant ecosystem of assets and financial applications."
> — **Walter Maffione, CTO, KaleidoSwap**

> "We are delighted to be an early investor into KaleidoSwap. We have been a long-time supporter of RGB and Lightning technologies, and are committed to making Bitcoin the most efficient transaction solution for everyone."
> — **Paolo Ardoino, CTO, Bitfinex**

> "KaleidoSwap's vision fully aligns with ours: build on Bitcoin, leverage Lightning for scalability, and use RGB for smart contracts. We continue to see enormous potential in a bitcoin-native technology stack and are proud to support the team in their mission."
> — **Oleg Mikhalsky, Fulgur Ventures**

### About KaleidoSwap

KaleidoSwap is pioneering the first decentralized exchange (DEX) built natively on **Bitcoin and the Lightning Network**. Leveraging **RGB**, an advanced protocol for smart contracts and asset issuance on Bitcoin, KaleidoSwap enables **trustless BTC–USDT trading** and a broader ecosystem of Bitcoin-backed assets. With a focus on scalability, privacy, and decentralization, KaleidoSwap is on a mission to become the **gateway for Bitcoin-native DeFi**.

### About Bitfinex Ventures

**Bitfinex Ventures** is the investment arm of Bitfinex, one of the world's longest-standing digital asset exchanges. The firm supports projects that advance the Bitcoin ecosystem and the broader blockchain industry, with a focus on infrastructure, scalability, and financial innovation. By investing in early-stage ventures, Bitfinex Ventures fosters technologies that align with its mission to make Bitcoin the most efficient global transaction solution.

### About Fulgur Ventures

Fulgur Ventures is a venture capital firm focused on Bitcoin technology and its applications and dedicated to accelerating the adoption of bitcoin as financial infrastructure.
